About

Verdant Palm Management LLC is a Wyoming-organized holding and management company. We steward capital, govern subsidiaries, and provide strategic oversight across select ventures in technology, energy, and digital infrastructure. Our mandate prioritizes risk management, compliance, and long-horizon value creation.

Scope of Activities

  • Capital allocation and treasury management (long-term positions)
  • Corporate governance and inter-company controls
  • Strategic planning and institutional relationships
  • IP and data-room stewardship for portfolio initiatives
  • Oversight of operating agreements and compliance frameworks
  • Internal services provided via operating affiliates

Corporate Structure
